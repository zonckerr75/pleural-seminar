Venue

The 2025 Pleural Seminar will be held at The Parkway Hotel & Spa in Cwmbran, Torfaen. All sessions take place in the Wentwood Suite.

Address

The Parkway Hotel & Spa
Cwmbran Drive, Cwmbran, Torfaen, NP44 3UW
Tel: 01633 871199
Email: enquiries@theparkwayhotel.co.uk

Open in Google Maps

Arrival & Timings

  • Registration: opens at 08:40 (foyer outside Wentwood Suite).
  • Refreshments: tea & coffee during breaks; lunch provided.

Parking

Ample free parking is available on the hotel grounds for all delegates.

Wi-Fi

Network (SSID): Guestwifi
Password: Parkway1

Tip: you can also scan the QR code to connect on most phones.

Directions

From M4 J26 (Eastbound)
Take the A4051 towards Cwmbran, continue through the traffic lights to the roundabout. Take the 1st exit onto Cwmbran Drive. At the next roundabout, take the 3rd exit into Llantarnam Park. Turn first right, then first left into the hotel entrance.

From M4 J25A (Westbound)
Take the A4051 towards Cwmbran and follow signs for Llantarnam Park. At the roundabout, take the 3rd exit onto Cwmbran Drive. Continue to the next roundabout and take the 3rd exit into Llantarnam Park. Turn right, then first left into the hotel entrance.
