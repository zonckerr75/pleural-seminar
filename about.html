Objectives

Pleural Seminar Wales is held annually each November and organised by the Wales Pleural Group, a collaborative network supported by the Welsh Thoracic Society and the wider NHS Wales respiratory community.

  • Strengthen the national network of clinicians involved in pleural medicine.
  • Share learning from Welsh and UK initiatives such as Project Cymru and the Mesothelioma Network Cymru.
  • Promote consistent, high-quality pleural care across all Welsh Health Boards.
  • Support service development through practical education, case-based discussion, and cross-site collaboration.
