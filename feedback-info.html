Feedback & Attendance Certificates

Thank you for joining the Welsh Pleural Seminar 2025.

To provide feedback and download your attendance certificate, please scan the unique QR code on your badge.

Scanning your badge QR code will open a secure feedback form linked to your registration. Once you have completed and submitted the feedback form, you will be able to:

  • Download your attendance certificate immediately, and
  • Optionally request a copy to be emailed to you.

If you are unable to submit feedback or cannot download your certificate, please contact us atAli.thahseen@wales.nhs.uk.

Thank you for taking the time to provide feedback — it helps us improve future seminars.
